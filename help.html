How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

How can I update my account information?

You can update your account info by logging into your account and going to the "Profile" section in the settings menu.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team by visiting the "Contact Support" section at the bottom of this page.
